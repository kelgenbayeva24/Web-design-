California

The California is a wellness, spa and beauty center. Refresh you mind, body and soul at the California with one of their signature treatments that range from 30 minutes to 5 hours. Choose from body and facial treatments including relaxation and deep tissue massage – the trained professional staff members focus on individual needs – should you need attention focused on your upper back and neck from computer strain, or on tired overworked legs and feet – the massage therapists will make you feel brand new.
